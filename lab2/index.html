The Patronus Charm

A defensive charm that conjures a protective guardian to shield the castor from dementors or to deliver messages.

The incantation translates to "I await a patron" and is derived from the Latin words expecto, meaning "I'm, ready and waiting," and patronus, meaning "mighty protector".

The patronus can take the form of a corporeal animal or an incorporeal wisp of silver. They are unique to each caster and can represent part of their character or a loved one.

Incantation: Expecto Patronum (ex-PEK-toh pa-TRO-num)
